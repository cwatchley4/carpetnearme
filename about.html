    Our Mission

    The purpose of Carpet Near Me is to provide excellent service for an affordable price in Dekalb, IL and Chicagoland. We strive to redefine spaces with top-notch flooring, ensuring a perfect blend of quality, comfort, and affordability. Your satisfaction is our priority as we aim to make every step of your flooring journey seamless and cost-effective.

    Our Values

    At Carpet Near Me, our values are anchored in passion and honesty. We take pride in being a flooring company dedicated to installing top-quality products with unparalleled craftsmanship. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every installation reflects our core values, providing you with the utmost satisfaction and trust in our services.

    Our Story

    Adrian, the founder of Carpet Near Me, has been in construction since 2001. With a vision to establish a lasting legacy for his family, Adrian opened his own business. He aimed to show to his family that through hard work and determination, one can create a business providing passionate service to others in a chosen career.

    Why Choose Us?

    We offer carpet and luxury vinyl plank flooring in residential and commerical applications. Luxury vinyl plank flooring is a durable and stylish synthetic flooring material that mimics the look of natural wood, offering a cost-effective and low-maintenance alternative to hardwood.
