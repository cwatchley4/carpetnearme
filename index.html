    Our Four Step Process

    At Carpet Near Me, we are committed to providing our customers with a seamless and exceptional experience from start to finish. Our utmost priority is your satisfaction, and we strive to make your journey with us as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

    Book Online

    Consultation

    At Carpet Near Me, we bring the store to your door. After you book a free consultation, we'll come to your house or business to discuss requirements, preferences, and budget. We'll do our best to provide recommendations based on your needs, showing you our samples, textures, colors, and patterns.​

    Estimate

    After selecting a carpet, we'll schedule an appointment to measure your space. A professional measurement ensures accurate carpet estimation and helps determine the cost of materials and installation. We will then provide you with a detailed estimate, including the carpet price, padding, installation charges, and any additional services requested.

    Installation

    Once the carpet arrives, the installation is scheduled and a professional installation team will visit your location at the agreed upon time and date. The team removes any existing carpet, prepared the subfloor, and installs new carpet according to industry standards. The installation team ensures a neat and professional finish, paying attention to details such as seams and transitions. ​

    Follow Up

    After installation, we'll provide you with information on how to care for and maintain the new carpet and we'll address any concerns or questions you may have post-installation.​
